Getting to Chicago

Chicago is our kind of town — big-city energy, neighborhood charm, and the best food you'll ever eat next to a lake that looks like an ocean. Fly into O'Hare (ORD) or Midway (MDW); both connect downtown by train. The venue sits in West Town, with Wicker Park, West Loop, and the Loop all close by and full of great places to stay, eat, and wander.
